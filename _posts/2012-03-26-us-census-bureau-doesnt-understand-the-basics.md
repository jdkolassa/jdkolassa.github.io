Emphasis mine.

It&#8217;s really not that hard to explain why, so I&#8217;ll lay it out for you:

  1. Lower taxes.
  2. Less regulation.
  3. Freer markets.

All these things combined lead to one thing: jobs. (Well, actually, they lead to a lot of things, but jobs are at the top of the list.) People are moving to the South and the West because there are jobs there. Of course, this is hard for government officials to grok (Wait, you mean they _don&#8217;t_ need us?) but for any person with two-thirds of a brain, it&#8217;s just plain common sense.

Man, I have got to find a job in Texas.
