For the first time on this blog, I am now using an SEO plugin. One of the cool things it does is redirect links to media attachments to their parent post/page. I&#8217;ve actually noticed people coming to view attachments on some of my older posts, which is kinda weird.

This also means I will be putting some sort of share plugin back in as well. I&#8217;ve avoided it for some time because I was convinced that &#8220;begging&#8221; for shares wasn&#8217;t the best thing in the world. Now I realize that was stupid.

I&#8217;m also on the lookout for a new theme for this blog. I like &#8220;required&#8221; (the current setup) but the stuff coming out of the marketplace for the Ghost platform fascinates me. There&#8217;s a few themes like that for WordPress I want to at least take a look at.

Another thing I want to try is using Scrivener as a WordPress blogging client. So far, there&#8217;s no direct &#8220;post to WordPress&#8221; function, just a Markdown export which can be translated into HTML. I must admit, I&#8217;m not terribly keen on Markdown, even though I have friends who rave about it. I just don&#8217;t see the point, especially for longer form works (which, as one of said friends is a novelist, is applicable.) I just do CTRL+I or whatever to get what I want. But I&#8217;ll leave that for later. I _have_ managed to figure out how to make Scrivener work &#8220;in the cloud&#8221; using Dropbox, which is really, really neat.

I must admit, this post is completely non-SEO, and is largely something of filler.
