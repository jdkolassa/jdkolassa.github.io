In a sign that I am really, really out of step with the mainstream, I have just discovered that this excrable piece of poodoo exists:



I know what you&#8217;re saying. &#8220;Oh, come on, it&#8217;s all in good fun.&#8221; And yes, I can buy that argument&#8230;up to a point. But there is a line upon which, if one crosses, it is no longer good fun. It is just stupid.

Pray tell, would you ever see a game where Sigourney Weaver would have to win a dancing competition, and that if she lost, the Xenomorph would get to eat her? Of course not. And I&#8217;m not arguing that _Star Wars_ is the same thing as _Alien_&#8211;they are about as far as one can get while staying in the science fiction genre&#8211;but they do share some thresholds. This one was crossed.

Han Solo would _never_ do those dances. Ever. Seeing him perform the &#8220;Trash Compactor&#8221; is just degrading. Leia I can see, yes (though she&#8217;d probably shoot you for making her do that), <del>or maybe even Mara Jade</del> (er, second thought, no.) But Han Solo is _not_, and never will be, some pop star singer/dancer that Neyo has signed on to his record. That&#8217;s not what his character is.

There is a part of its that charming and hilarious (I actually like the song they sing, by the way) but something about this deeply and inexorable grates on my soul. And I just don&#8217;t know how to say it, other than: THIS SUCKS.
