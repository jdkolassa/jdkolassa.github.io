I&#8217;m extremely pleased to announce that I am participating in the May 2013 edition of Cato Unbound ([@CatoUnbound](http://twitter.com/CatoUnbound)), the most intelligent online journal of intellectualism.

The topic of this month is fusionism, specifically between libertarians and conservatives. My good friend and America&#8217;s Iron Lady, Jacque Otto ([@jacque_otto](http://twitter.com/jacque_otto)) is kicking off with a lead essay, followed by yours truly on Wednesday, to then be followed by Students for Liberty Vice President Clark Ruper ([@clark_ruper](http://twitter.com/clark_ruper)) on Friday and Acton Institute Research Fellow Jordan Ballor ([@JordanBallor](http://twitter.com/JordanBallor)) on Monday.

This is the big leagues, folks, and I am very proud to be here. While six years ago I wanted to just do sci-fi writing, this is still extremely exciting. And I&#8217;m sure I can work it into my science fiction&#8211;after all, a great many science fiction writers were and are passionate libertarians. For that reason, HUGE thanks are in order to Cato Unbound editor [@JasonKuznicki](http://twitter.com/JasonKuznicki), to whom I now owe a keg of scotch. Or something.

Please read the lead essay up here, and feel free to join in the discussion!
