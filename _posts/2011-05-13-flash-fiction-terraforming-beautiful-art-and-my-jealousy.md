[I am sorry you have to live in the time of terraforming, and not in the spring that follows](http://io9.com/5801149/i-am-sorry-you-have-to-live-in-the-time-of-terraforming-and-not-in-the-spring-that-follows/gallery/).

I seriously don&#8217;t know what&#8217;s more beautiful here: the artwork presented in this post, or the flash fiction that accompanies it. I am truly jealous to both parties; jealous that my visual art skills are, to be honest, just bloody awful, and jealous that I am incapable of condensing fiction into something that small. It&#8217;s only 235 words, very concise. I suppose, for me, the problem is that I don&#8217;t see it as a story: there&#8217;s no real beginning, middle, or end, no real plot, nothing moves, truly. It is merely a vignette, a sketch of some far off land.

That gives me ideas, though. Maybe if I focus less on plot and just on place, setting..hmm&#8230;ideas, percolating&#8230;

But yes, I am insanely jealous of both. And no, I don&#8217;t think jealousy is a bad thing. It pushes us to be better, to improve, to evolve. I just wish it wouldn&#8217;t come with the side effects.
