So Script Frenzy&#8211;the screenwriting equivalent of National Novel Writing Month&#8211;has begun. 100 pages in 30 days. That&#8217;s roughly 3 1/3 pages a day. Fortunately, for day one, I wrote six, meaning I am 6% completed.

Yes, yes, I know that you probably can do the math better than I can. I&#8217;m just reveling in it, because I know this success probably won&#8217;t last much longer. But for now&#8230;bed.

Oh, and if you&#8217;re wondering, I&#8217;m working on the screenplay equivalent of a novella I had been thinking about and sort of worked on since junior high school. Originally, my idea was to write the novella first, get that published, and then try to sell the screenplay so I could then base it off of that (I heard it helped to sell screenplays based on already existing written works), but I&#8217;m not sure any more. I can certainly still do that, though, but once I finish this first.
