I want to just something really quick here: fashion is stupid. Seriously, have you see all these people walking around, trying to be cool, wearing the latest from Brooks Brothers and Nieman Marcus and whatever. And of course, loads and loads of fashion advice, [like this, bolded emphasis mine](http://dailycaller.com/2011/06/29/more-summer-must-haves/):

> When American [<span style="color: green;">penny loafers</span>](http://dailycaller.com/2011/06/29/more-summer-must-haves/#){#KonaLink3}, a staple of the Ivy League, were first introduced in the 1930s, they were called “Norwegian Loafers” because of their design’s Norwegian origins. This is why they are nicknamed “Weejuns,” although the Connecticut-based shoemaker Bass Weejuns remains the maker of the _real_ __penny loafer. Penny loafers can be worn with or without socks, depending on the season — **although in the summer, it’s _de riguer_ to wear them without socks.** They can be worn with a blazer, khakis, cords or jeans, but never with a suit, unless you’re a [<span style="color: green;">college student</span>](http://dailycaller.com/2011/06/29/more-summer-must-haves/#){#KonaLink4}. (The loafer-suit combination is just too formal.) Penny loafers look best in brown or cordovan; you should never wear black ones. Replace the soles and heels as they wear out. Clean them with saddle soap and polish them regularly with a wax-based polish until the leather has a gleaming, cracked patina that says you’ve owned them forever.

Can someone tell me how that even looks remotely acceptable? [If you&#8217;re a guy and look like this](http://www.google.com/search?q=loafers+without+socks&hl=en&client=firefox-a&hs=Tic&rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&prmd=ivns&tbm=isch&tbo=u&source=univ&sa=X&ei=DUELToisLaX40gHugpyqAQ&ved=0CDEQsAQ&biw=1280&bih=651), you look like a bum. You look like you can&#8217;t afford socks. You look like you should have a half-empty bottle of red wine in your hand while you sway gently from one side of the sidewalk to the other. In short, it&#8217;s stupid. (Obviously, if you&#8217;re a woman you can wear whatever the hell you want, because you&#8217;re a woman, and we&#8217;ll drool all over you even if you wrapped yourself in broccoli.)

If there&#8217;s any truth to the &#8220;social decay&#8221; that social conservatives are constantly ranting about, its this. Its tacky, plain and simple.
