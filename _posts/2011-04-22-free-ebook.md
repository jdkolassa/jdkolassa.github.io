[Amazon.com: Do the Work (9781936719013): Steven Pressfield: Books](http://www.amazon.com/Do-Work-Steven-Pressfield/dp/1936719010?pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=center-1&pf_rd_r=0W5J2P0EAN1P4A2GNJCB&pf_rd_t=1401&pf_rd_p=1293397342&pf_rd_i=1000673581).

This is more of a bookmark for myself than anything else, and even though I have it in Instapaper and I starred the InstaPundit entry that featured it in my Google Reader, I figure, hey, if I&#8217;m going to remind myself to get a free ebook, I should probably go whole hog.

From what I understand, _Do The Work_ is a book on just getting stuff done, and it [has rave reviews](http://pajamasmedia.com/instapundit/119078/). The best part, though, is that its being released _for free_ on Amazon Kindle&#8211;or any one of its numerous apps for various platforms&#8211;up until May 20th, 2011. Since its normal price is $7.79, I think that&#8217;s a sweet deal. I myself could very well use a book on just this topic.

An interesting thing to note is why its free (emphasis mine):

> <span style="font-size: 0.82em;"><span class="qpHeadline">Read &#8220;Do the Work&#8221; for Free, Courtesy of GE</span><br /> Want to read <em><a href="http://www.amazon.com/Do-the-Work-ebook/dp/B004PGO25O/ref=amb_link_355688682_1?pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=hero-quick-promo&pf_rd_r=05H6K5WFV8EEQ9X0FQT5&pf_rd_t=201&pf_rd_p=1291976662&pf_rd_i=1936719010">Do the Work</a></em> for free? <strong>Thanks to a generous sponsorship by General Electric</strong>, the Kindle ebook edition of <em>Do the Work</em> is available for free through May 20, 2011. No Kindle? <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html/ref=amb_link_355688682_2?ie=UTF8&docId=1000493771&pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=hero-quick-promo&pf_rd_r=05H6K5WFV8EEQ9X0FQT5&pf_rd_t=201&pf_rd_p=1291976662&pf_rd_i=1936719010">Download</a> a free Kindle app for PC, Mac, iPhone, iPad, Blackberry, Windows Phone 7, or Android today. </span>

GE, huh? Just sponsoring authors to give books away, huh? I guess that&#8217;s why they got off on taxes. Now, I don&#8217;t support big corporations being able to get out of paying taxes while the rest of us can&#8217;t, but it does do damage to the image that corporations are greedy leviathans that do no good. I mean, they&#8217;re giving you _free books_ for crying out loud. What can be better than that?

I should note, as an aside, I have gotten some work done this week, and intend to do more. I have also been busy with several other things this week, namely birthday parties. Those are always fun.
