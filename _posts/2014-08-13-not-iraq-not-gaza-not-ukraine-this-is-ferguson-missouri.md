Meanwhile, <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/08/12/journalists-threatened-michael-brown_n_5671155.html?&ir=Politics&ncid=tweetlnkushpmg00000016" target="_blank">the local government has completely shut reporters out of the city</a>:

> Journalists encountered a threatening response from police as they tried to cover the protests in Ferguson, the Missouri town that has been upended by the <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/08/10/michael-brown_n_5665935.html?utm_hp_ref=black-voices" target="_hplink">police killing of Michael Brown</a>, an unarmed black teenager.
> 
> While there was a spate of looting on Sunday night, Monday&#8217;s demonstrations were <a href="http://www.stltoday.com/news/local/crime-and-courts/police-use-tear-gas-in-ferguson-people-jam-church-for/article_6060a563-d7d1-544a-8bb3-793c46be427c.html" target="_hplink">peaceful</a>. Protestors <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/news/post-nation/wp/2014/08/12/police-use-tear-gas-on-crowd/" target="_hplink">faced tear gas and rubber bullets</a> from officers trying to break their ranks up. At the same time, police told local media to get out of the area.

This is not America. This is the purview of a third world tinpot dictatorship, not the leader of the free world and the greatest democracy in the world. Period. They do not need to be <a href="http://www.theblaze.com/stories/2014/08/09/two-cops-are-stomping-a-prone-suspect-what-a-third-cop-does-when-he-pulls-up-to-the-scene-has-him-facing-10-years-in-jail/?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=story&utm_campaign=ShareButtons" target="_blank">stomping people just laying on the ground</a>, or engaging in illegal chokehold manuevers to <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2014/08/01/justice/new-york-choke-hold-death/" target="_blank">kill a man who was only selling untaxed cigarettes</a>. Or getting a warrant to <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/national/n-m-man-settles-1-6-million-anally-probed-8-times-routine-traffic-stop-article-1.1582589" target="_blank">conduct a horribly invasive anal probe</a> _eight times_ for &#8220;drugs&#8221; on the flimsiest of evidence. Or firing <a href="http://www.gopusa.com/freshink/2013/11/19/n-mexico-police-fired-at-van-full-of-kids-driven-by-uncooperative-mother/" target="_blank">dozens of rounds through a van full of children</a>. Or <a href="http://www.policemisconduct.net/" target="_blank">any other terrible acts by police</a>.

Slowly, over the past several years, the police have been transformed from a law enforcement agency to a sort of Russian style &#8220;Internal Troops&#8221; division. Do we really want to import Putinism over here to the United States?

It is _past_ time for Americans to wake up to this and demand action. Demand that police face the full consequences of their actions. Demand that &#8220;paid administrative leave&#8221; be ended. Demand the mindlessly stupid War on Drugs is declared over, and demand that out of control cops are reigned back in. Demand that <a href="http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/aug/12/nypd-body-worn-camera-pilot-chokehold-death" target="_blank">cop cameras be used everywhere</a>, and can&#8217;t be altered or lost by the cops they cover.

This madness needs to end. We are the nation that leads the free world. Time to act like it.
