I am in full favor of [morphologial freedom](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morphological_freedom). I support everyone&#8217;s [right to self-ownership](http://bleedingheartlibertarians.com/2012/11/self-ownership-an-uncontroversial-conclusion-among-liberals-not-a-controversial-premise/), which includes the power to change one&#8217;s gender. I do not take issue with transgendered or transsexual Americans. [I am not a conservative](http://www.cato-unbound.org/2013/05/07/jeremy-kolassa/unequal-treaty), and do not buy conservative arguments that one cannot change that. If we are to respect people as individuals, then that must include the power over one&#8217;s morphology.

That being said, [in regards to Bradley Manning now declaring he is a woman and to be called Chelsea](http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2013/08/22/214440560/bradley-manning-i-am-a-female-call-me-chelsea), just as he&#8217;s being sentenced to prison&#8230;aren&#8217;t you supposed to become a bitch while _in_ prison, not before?
