Just wanted to say that, if Newt Gingrich wins the GOP nomination, Gary Johnson is going to get some serious support. He&#8217;s not going to win, mind you, and might not even get any electoral votes, but he&#8217;ll pick up some decent support from the public. Newt is the mirror image of Obama. He doesn&#8217;t stand for anything other than himself. He&#8217;s a mendacious toad, as my friend Doug Mataconis [described](https://twitter.com/#!/dmataconis/status/160014738292609024) [him](https://twitter.com/#!/dmataconis/status/160009293742084097). The guy is positively toxic. If the GOP nominates him, then it deserves to be electorally eliminated as a party, and replaced with something else. Maybe that will be the Libertarian Party. I don&#8217;t know. (Probably not, they have issues.) But if Newt gets the nomination, I will bet dollars to donuts that Gary Johnson, _if_ the Libertarian Party is intelligent enough to select him as its nominee, with get around 5% of the popular vote, give or take a couple of points (so, 3-7%). He&#8217;s the only one standing up for civil liberties, who is against the war, against drug prohibition, for seriously reducing the size of government, restoring some sanity, and doesn&#8217;t have any racist baggage or come off as a crank.
