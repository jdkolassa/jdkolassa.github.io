If Livejournal is not deceiving me (which, considering all the DDoS attacks they&#8217;ve had of late, it&#8217;s possible), then today is Aaron Allston&#8217;s birthday.

Allston is one of my all time favorite writers, having written the _Star Wars: X-Wing_ arc for Wraith Squadron, as well as more books in the _Fate of the Jedi_ series. (I&#8217;ll leave him off the hook for that one; his writing his awesome, it&#8217;s the overall continuity direction I&#8217;m disappointed in.) His books are based on sharp dialogue, even sharper wit, and deep character interactions. Also, his work is hilarious. No, literally, it&#8217;s so damn funny I laugh even the 100th time I&#8217;m reading it.

Another good point is that he really proves that &#8220;media tie-in authors&#8221; are just as important and skilled as those who write original fiction, which is something that interests me. (I mean, heck, who _wouldn&#8217;t_ want to write an official _Star Wars_ novel and get paid for it? Only a loony, that&#8217;s for sure.)

Happy Birthday, Mr. Allston. I look forward to many more years of your work.

(And if this is _not_ your birthday, well, I guess Frank the LJ Goat will have some &#8216;splaining to do!)
