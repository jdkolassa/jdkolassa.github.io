Well, as you can clearly see, I have a new website theme up and running. And, if you&#8217;ve viewing on a smartphone or a tablet, you now get a special mobile theme. It might not be the prettiest thing, but it is functional. 

Lately, I&#8217;ve wanted to update my blog design. The Twenty Eleven theme was functional, and with the dark color scheme and blue banner, I actually thought it looked quite cool, but it felt kind of&#8230;I&#8217;m not sure. Pedestrian? Goth? I had one viewer complain of white on black text. While I disagree that&#8217;s a serious visibility issue (plenty of people like light text on dark background) I did want to shake things up. So I adopted this theme ([&#8220;Required&#8221;](http://blog.8bit.io/required/)), popped on some Open Sans for legibility, and went to town.

That&#8217;s what you get when you develop some web development skills. You start thinking you can make your blog all pretty and whatnot&#8230;

I have more content coming for this, including a few new pages (!!!) and of course more blog posts, and hopefully more fiction as well. I&#8217;m not sure how much I want to comment on my personal life on this blog&#8211;this is not my Livejournal*, after all&#8211;but I have had many reasons to become far more optimistic and hopeful towards the future in the last few months. I have made many friends, and more importantly, I think I&#8217;ve actually found a niche. I&#8217;m developing skills I will use for a long time, and I don&#8217;t just mean the technical ones. My life is also acquiring a bit more stability, and I think, while time management will continue to vex me, I will be able to handle more things thrown my way from now on.

I&#8217;ll still be tweaking this site&#8211;if enough people complain about the color scheme I&#8217;ll change that, and the Facebook button on the side doesn&#8217;t work because it&#8217;s supposed to link to a Facebook page, which setting up is problematic&#8211;but for now, I&#8217;m pretty pleased.

Welcome to the new QMS. Same as the old QMS, really, just with a better design.</p>
